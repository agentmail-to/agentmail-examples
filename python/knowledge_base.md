# Knowledge Base

Common questions and answers the agent can resolve directly. Replace these with your own product's FAQs — keep them short, specific, and accurate. The agent stuffs this whole file into the system prompt at startup, so don't make it huge (~30-50 entries is the sweet spot before the prompt gets noisy).

Format: `## Question` headings followed by the answer. Anything outside this format is ignored.

---

## How do I cancel my subscription?

Log in, go to **Settings → Billing → Cancel subscription**. Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing period — you keep access until then. No prorated refunds, but no further charges either.

## What payment methods do you support?

All major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover) plus ACH bank transfers for annual plans. We don't currently support PayPal or crypto.

## How do I update my billing address or card?

**Settings → Billing → Update payment method.** Changes apply to the next invoice — current invoices are not retroactively updated.

## I was charged twice / wrong amount — can I get a refund?

Refunds for billing errors are processed within 5-7 business days once verified. Email a screenshot of the duplicate charge and we'll investigate. Note: agents cannot authorize refunds directly — these always go to a human reviewer.

## How do I get an invoice / receipt for a past payment?

**Settings → Billing → Invoice history.** Click any line item to download the PDF receipt.

## How do I change my plan / upgrade / downgrade?

**Settings → Billing → Change plan.** Upgrades take effect immediately (prorated). Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing period.

## I forgot my password.

Visit the login page and click "Forgot password" — a reset link is emailed to the address on file. Links expire after 1 hour.

## How do I add team members to my workspace?

**Settings → Team → Invite member.** Available on Team and Enterprise plans only. Solo plan is single-user.

## How do I export my data?

**Settings → Data → Export.** Generates a ZIP of all your content as JSON + CSV, emailed to you when ready (usually within an hour for accounts under 10GB).

## Is my data encrypted?

Yes — TLS in transit, AES-256 at rest. We're SOC 2 Type II certified. Full security details at our trust portal (link in footer).

## Can I delete my account?

Yes. **Settings → Account → Delete account.** This is permanent — all data is wiped within 30 days and cannot be recovered. We retain billing records for 7 years per legal requirements.
