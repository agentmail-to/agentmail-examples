## Show HN Post

**Title:** Show HN: Forward a contract to an AI agent, get a redline review back

**Body:**
Built an agent that reviews contracts against your standard terms and replies with a redline summary. Forward the contract text to its email, and it flags risky clauses: unlimited liability, auto-renewal, one-sided indemnity, IP assignment.

Not a replacement for legal counsel. A first-pass filter that catches the obvious issues in seconds.

Configure your standard terms in a JSON file. The agent compares each contract against them.

AgentMail (https://agentmail.to) + OpenAI. Python, ~200 lines.

Repo: https://github.com/agentmail-to/contract-redline-agent
